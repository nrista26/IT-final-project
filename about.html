🌷 A Little About Me 🌷

♡ Who Am I?

Hi! I'm Rista, and welcome to ristagotdoxed. This is my personal travel and photography blog where I share the places I've visited, the photos I've taken, and the memories I've made along the way. I love exploring new places, capturing beautiful moments, and turning every trip into a story worth remembering.

📸 Why I Started This Blog

Every time I travel, I end up taking hundreds of photos that usually stay hidden in my camera roll. I wanted a special place where I could keep my favourite memories together, write about my experiences, and share them with others. This website became my own little online travel diary filled with moments that make me smile.

✈️ What You'll Find Here

You'll find photos from different destinations, short travel stories, beautiful scenery, and little moments that made each trip unforgettable. Whether it's a peaceful mountain view or a busy city street, every picture has its own story.
